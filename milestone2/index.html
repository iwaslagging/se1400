Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

NightQuest will have ideas for fun things college students can do at night or on the weekend. It will include local events, food spots, places to hang out, date ideas, and other fun things to do near campus.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

I will write most of the text myself based on what the website is about. The images will come from free stock photo websites, and I might also use some placeholder images while I am still designing the site.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users should care because a lot of college students want something fun to do but do not always know where to go. This site would make it easier to find ideas without having to search all over the place.

Why should a site be built for this?

A site should be built for this because students are always looking for fun, affordable things to do with their friends. A website like this would put everything in one place and make it faster and easier to plan something.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I want users to come to the site to find places to go, get ideas for the weekend, and figure out what sounds fun based on what they like and how much money they want to spend.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of NightQuest is to help college students find fun and affordable things to do. I want it to be a simple guide people can use when they are bored, want to go out, or need ideas for the weekend.
