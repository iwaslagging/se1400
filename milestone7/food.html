Food & Drinks

Because every good night starts with knowing where to eat. Cheap, delicious, and close to campus.

🍕 Cheap Eats

Good food doesn't have to drain your dining account. These spots keep your wallet and your stomach happy.

Taco Tuesday (Every Day)

Street-style tacos under $3 each at a local taqueria near campus. The al pastor and carne asada are legendary. Order three, thank yourself later. Cash only — there's an ATM inside.

Under $10 🌮 Mexican

Dollar Slice Pizza

New York-style by-the-slice pizza available until midnight near the student union. Grab a slice for $1.50–$2.00, fold it in half like a civilized person, and get back to your night. Cheese, pepperoni, and a rotating special slice always available.

$1.50–$4 🍕 Pizza

Campus Burger Shack

Smash burgers done right. Double smash with cheese, caramelized onions, and their signature sauce runs under $8. Add fries for $2 more. Lines get long after 9pm on Fridays, so plan accordingly or go early.

$6–$12 🍔 Burgers

🌙 Late-Night Food

It's 1am and somehow you're hungry again. We get it. Here's where to go.

Midnight Ramen

A ramen shop open until 2am on weekends. Rich broth, handmade noodles, and customizable toppings. The spicy miso ramen with a soft-boiled egg is the go-to post-event meal. Warm, filling, and surprisingly affordable at around $12 a bowl.

$10–$15 🍜 Ramen

Loaded Fries Cart

A late-night food cart that sets up downtown around 10pm on weekends. Loaded fries with toppings like pulled pork, cheese sauce, jalapeños, or buffalo chicken. Portions are huge and prices are fair. Follow them on Instagram for exact locations.

$7–$10 🍟 Street Food

24/7 Diner

The classic late-night diner experience. Open 24 hours, serves breakfast all day (and night), and has a full menu of comfort food. Pancakes at 3am? Yes. Greasy hash browns? Absolutely. Bring cash for faster service and leave a good tip — those night shift workers deserve it.

$8–$15 🥞 Diner

☕ Study-Friendly Cafes

When you need to caffeinate AND procrastinate at the same time, these spots have you covered.

Campus Corner Cafe

Strong wifi, plenty of outlets, and a chill playlist that actually lets you focus. Open until midnight most nights. The cold brew is excellent. No pressure to leave after buying one drink — they're student-friendly like that.

⭐ 4.6  •  $  •  Open until midnight

Free WiFi

The Roast Room

A specialty coffee shop a few blocks off campus. They roast their own beans in-house and the menu rotates seasonally. A little pricier than your average chain, but the atmosphere and quality make it worth it for a focused afternoon session.

⭐ 4.9  •  $$  •  Open until 10pm

Specialty Coffee

🍴 Food Tips for Students

  • Many restaurants near campus offer a hidden student discount — just ask when you order. You'd be surprised how often it works.
  • Download apps like Google Maps and filter by "open now" to find late-night options when you're already out.
  • Food truck events are almost always the best bang for your buck — free entry, variety, and a fun atmosphere.
  • Splitting a large order with your group is almost always cheaper per person than everyone ordering separately.
  • Follow local food spots on Instagram — they often post flash deals, pop-up hours, and free item giveaways.
