Events This Weekend

From live music to local festivals — find something actually worth leaving the couch for.

🎉 This Weekend's Events

Handpicked activities happening right now near campus. Check local listings for exact times and locations.

Indie Night Live

A rotating lineup of local indie and alternative bands hits the main stage every Friday. Expect raw energy, tight setlists, and a crowd that's actually into the music. Student IDs get you $3 off at the door.

$8 🎸 Live Music

Open Mic Night

Every Thursday, the campus arts center opens its stage to anyone with a mic and a dream. Singer-songwriters, comedians, poets — all welcome. Sign up at the door before 7pm. Audience entry is always free.

Free 🎤 Open Mic

DJ Night @ The Basement

The city's best underground club night brings rotating DJs spinning EDM, hip-hop, and bass music. Under-21 welcome until 11pm. After midnight it's 21+ only. Arrive early for free entry before 10pm.

$5–$10 🎧 Nightlife

💵 Free & Cheap Events

Broke student energy is real. These events cost little to nothing but deliver all the fun.

Food Truck Friday

Every Friday evening downtown, 15+ food trucks gather for a street food festival. No entry fee — just show up, walk around, and eat your heart out. Tacos, BBQ, loaded fries, desserts, and more. Bring cash and an appetite.

Free Entry 🌮 Food

Campus Movie Night

The student union puts on free outdoor movie screenings on select Saturday nights on the main lawn. Bring a blanket, grab cheap snacks from the table, and enjoy a film under the stars. Check the campus events board for titles.

Free 🎬 Movie

Wednesday Trivia Night

Multiple bars and restaurants around campus host trivia on Wednesday nights. Free to enter, teams of up to 6. Categories range from pop culture to sports history. Winners often take home bar tabs or gift cards. Arrive by 7pm to secure a spot.

Free 🧠 Trivia

👯 Group Events

Drag your whole crew out. These activities are best when you're rolling deep.

Bowling Night

Most local bowling alleys offer cheap group deals on weeknights — sometimes as low as $2 per game per person with shoe rental. Several locations near campus stay open until midnight. Perfect for groups of 4–8 who want low-stakes competition.

$2–$5/game 🎳 Group

Mini Golf at Fiesta Fun

Classic 18-hole mini golf, arcade games, go-karts, and laser tag all under one roof. This place is built for groups who want variety. Prices are budget-friendly, especially if you split an arcade card. Open late on weekends — perfect for impulsive night plans.

$7–$12 ⛳ Activities

Karaoke Bar Night

Two local venues run karaoke every Thursday and Saturday. Private rooms available for groups if reserved in advance, but the main floor karaoke is just as chaotic and fun. Expect top 40, throwbacks, and at least one person absolutely committing to Bohemian Rhapsody.

$3–$8 🎤 Karaoke

📋 Quick Event Tips

  • Always bring your student ID — many venues offer discounts or priority entry for enrolled students.
  • Check the event's social media or website the day of to confirm it's still happening. Schedules change.
  • Arrive 30–45 minutes early for popular events like trivia and karaoke to guarantee a table or spot.
  • Look for flyers around campus — local venues often post deals that aren't listed anywhere online.
  • Split rideshare costs with your group to keep the night affordable if you're heading downtown.
